# Educator Portal - Member Area

A premium, dark-mode educational member portal designed with a streaming-style interface to provide an exclusive experience for professors.

## Features Accomplished

- **Secure Common Access**: A clean and elegant login page (`index.html`) designed for shared credentials.
- **Streaming Dashboard**: A Netflix-inspired grid showing owned materials with progress bars and interactive hover states.
- **Upsell Strategy**: A "Locked" section using blur effects and padlock icons to encourage future purchases.
- **Internal Content Pages**: Dedicated module pages featuring PDF download capabilities and specific **Bonus Video Lessons** with high educational value.
- **Mobile Responsive**: Fully adaptive design that works seamlessly on desktops and mobile devices.
